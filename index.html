Our biggest installation yet. TESL8 is a 7.6-foot hexagonal sculpture built from 11 layers of CNC-cut marine plywood — brought to life with projection mapping and integrated LED lighting.

Built in collaboration with Maria Ingui and currently on display at The Medium in Springdale, AR through May 2026, supported by the Creative Exchange Fund art grant.

About

Rex Roberts

I'm a visual artist and co-founder of VJ Academy, a 600+ member international community where I teach the craft of live visual performance — known in the industry as VJing.

My work covers the full range — 2D and 3D digital art, CNC-cut sculpture, projection mapping, and live performance with LED walls and projectors. I build things by hand and bring them to life with light and code.

Based in Fayetteville, Arkansas. Currently going deep on projection mapping and interactive installations while continuing to teach and build at VJ Academy.
