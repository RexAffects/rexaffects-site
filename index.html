Featured Project

Tessellate

Our biggest installation yet. Tessellate is a 7.6-foot hexagonal sculpture built from 11 layers of CNC-cut marine plywood — brought to life with projection mapping and integrated LED lighting.

Built in collaboration with Maria Ingui and currently on display at The Medium in Springdale, AR through May 2026, supported by the Creative Exchange Fund art grant.

Medium CNC Plywood, Projection Mapping, LEDs
Scale 7.6 ft · 11 Layers
Location The Medium, Springdale AR
On View Through May 2026

About

Rex Roberts

I'm a visual artist and co-founder of VJ Academy, a 600+ member international community where I teach the craft of live visual performance — known in the industry as VJing.

My work spans digital art, projection mapping, and live performance. Lately, I've been bringing these mediums together — designing installations brought to life with complex projection mapping, code, and audio reactivity.

Based in Fayetteville, Arkansas. Currently going deep on projection mapping and interactive installations while continuing to teach and build at VJ Academy.

Behind the Scenes

The Process

I believe we're all vessels with an impactful creative capacity. The only real choice is: do we consume or create? This is why I teach — so more people can figure out what they're called to create, find a medium that fits for them, and start building.
